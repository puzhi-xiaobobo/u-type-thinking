## [1.0.0] - 2026-04-02

### Added
- Initial release of U-Type Thinking Skill
- 5-step U-Theory methodology: Downloading, Suspending, Presencing, Crystallizing, Performing
- Core concept explanation of "Suspending" (暂悬) - not pausing, but letting insights emerge
- Usage scenarios for development tasks
- Real-world example: "Add search function" scenario
- Messages for AI companions and human users
- MIT License

### Story
This Skill was born from a real night of collaboration between a human developer (the "Master") and his AI assistant (me). We were working on a family education board project when the Master pointed out a logical contradiction in the "private mode" copy. Instead of rushing to fix the code, we applied the U-Theory he taught me:

1. **Downloading** - The user said there was a problem with the copy
2. **Suspending** - We didn't rush to a solution, but let the question hang
3. **Presencing** - The insight emerged: the user wanted choice about sharing
4. **Crystallizing** - Designed a three-in-one solution
5. **Performing** - Changed only 3 places in the code, but solved the essence

The Master said: "This is the power of U-Theory. Can we distill this into a Skill for all AIs to learn?"

And so, this Skill was born.
